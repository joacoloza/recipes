Guacamole

Description

This guacamole recipe gets a tasty kick from cayenne and cilantro. You can serve it smooth or chunky depending on your tastes. Serve with homemade tortilla chips.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • Â½ cup diced onion
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic

How to

  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl;

  2. mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper.

  3. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
